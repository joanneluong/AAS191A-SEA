Where do Southeast Asian UCLA Students feel supported?

As part of our final project for AAS191 in Spring 2023, we surveyed Southeast Asian Students in UCLA to ask about their experiences in receiving support in UCLA. Hover over the markers in our map to explore the individual narratives of the Southeast Asian community in UCLA! Click on the 'RESPONSES' button to view full response from respondents or explore support locations on campus by clicking on the 'LOCATIONS' button.
